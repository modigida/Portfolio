Workout Tracker

This is a WPF application tailored for gym enthusiasts to track and analyze their workouts. The app uses the MVVM design pattern and integrates MongoDB for data storage and management.

Key Features:

  • Set Training Goals: Define weight goals for specific exercises.
  • Log Workouts: Record exercises, weights, and repetitions in real time.
  • Statistics: Visualize training data with pie charts and track progress towards personal goals.
  • Exercise Management: Create, edit, and delete exercises.
  • Personal Records: Automatically updated during workouts and dynamically managed.

Workouts:

  • Review past workouts or create new ones.
  • Automatically log session duration and calculate total lifted weight, sets, and repetitions during workouts.

Technologies:

  • WPF (Windows Presentation Foundation)
  • XAML
  • MongoDB & MongoDB Driver
  • MVVM
